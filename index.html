Your Custom Website

A career in the performing arts is incredibly difficult - getting a website shouldn't be.

My mission is simple: to build you an excellent, hassle-free website that showcases you in the best way possible.
As a full-time web developer, and actor myself, I know precisely what your website needs to deliver and how it can be done.

web development

Fast, sleek, modern sites fully customized to suit your needs.

mobile responsive

Your site will look and function just as well on a cell phone as it does on a desktop (and everything in between).

Update Content Yourself!

A built in content management system will allow you to easily make simple content updates to your site whenever you please.

Turnkey

I will hand off a completely finished website the requires no work on your part until you are ready to make updates!

How Does This Work?

Here is the basic gist, but we can get much deeper into the nitty-gritty details together.

Design

The first thing you will do is choose from a selection of fully-customizable designs. Customizable is the key word here. Whatever you choose will be coded and built to suit your needs. All available designs are fully responsive and mobile-device friendly.

Sit Back and Relax

Once a design is chosen and I have your wish-list in hand, I will do all the heavy lifting and build your website.

Finishing Touches

After the initial build, I'll have you jump back in and see if there are any final tweaks or changes you want made to your site. Once approved, your site will be live in no time!

What About Afterwards?

Odds are you will want to share exciting news and make other various updates to your site. I will include an easy-to-use content management system that allows you to make edits to the content on your website. Need a more substantial change? I'm only an email away, but I will also supply you with the source code for your website so any other developer can easily work on it.
