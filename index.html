How Does This Work?

Here is the basic gist, but we can get much deeper into the nitty-gritty details together.

Our Jumping Off Point

The first thing you will do is select from a list a fully-customizable design templates. Customizable is the key word here. All available designs are fully responsize and mobile-device friendly.

Sit Back and Relax

Once a design is chosen and I have your wish-list in hand, I will do all the heavy lifting and build your website.

Finishing Touches

After the initial build, I'll have you jump back in and see if there are any final tweaks or changes you want made to your site. Once approved, your site will be live in no time!

What About Afterwards?

Odds are you will want to share exciting news and make other various updates to your site. I will include a built in content management system that allows you to make edits to what is on your website. Need a more substantial change? I'm only an email away, but I will also supply you with the source code for your website so any other developer can easily work on it.

Shane O'Neill

Full-Time Web Developer / Reformed Actor / Snorkeling Enthusiast

Uniquely qualified to build websites for performers, Shane holds undergraduate degrees in Musical Theatre and Electronic Media Production, as well as a Masters of Music in Vocal Performance/Musical Theatre. In addition to his time on the stage, Shane spent a number of years as an Adjunct Professor of Theatre and private voice instructor. Ultimately, as fate would have it, Shane pivoted careers at the age of 31 and has been working full-time as a professional web developer ever since (while still sneaking back onto the stage any chance he gets).
